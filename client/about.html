About Poorni Gems

Three decades of excellence in the gemstone industry

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by renowned gemologist Dr. Lakshan Jayaweera, Poorni Gems began as a small gem trading company in Mawanelle, Sri Lanka. Dr. Lakshan's vision was to create a marketplace that combined traditional gemstone knowledge with modern business practices.

Over the years, we've grown into a global leader in gemstone trading, mining, and equipment supply, while maintaining our commitment to ethical sourcing and quality.
